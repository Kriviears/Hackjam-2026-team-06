# 🧭 Compass Product Roadmap

## 🌟 Vision Statement

Compass is envisioned as the intelligent career navigation platform for the Per Scholas ecosystem.

Rather than serving as a one-time AI roadmap generator, Compass will evolve into a lifelong career companion that empowers learners to navigate every stage of their professional journey—from exploring technology careers, to completing technical training, securing employment, advancing professionally, and returning to upskill throughout their careers.

Our vision is to create a connected ecosystem where AI transforms learner data into personalized guidance, actionable insights, and meaningful career opportunities.

---

# 🚀 Phase 1 — AI Career Navigation (MVP) ✅

The current Compass MVP establishes the foundation by providing:

- 🧠 AI-generated personalized career roadmaps
- 🗺️ Interactive roadmap visualization
- 📊 Personalized learner dashboard
- ⏱️ Adaptive pace recommendations
- 📚 AI-generated learning resources
- 💼 Future You career opportunities
- 🤖 Ask Compass AI assistant
- 📈 Progress and milestone tracking

---

# 🎓 Phase 2 — Connected Learning Experience

Compass will integrate more deeply with the learner experience by connecting to Per Scholas educational systems.

### Future capabilities include:

- 📖 Automatic course completion tracking
- 🔄 Dynamic roadmap updates as milestones are completed
- 📅 Learning progress synchronization
- 🎯 Personalized study recommendations
- 🗓️ Calendar integration
- 🔔 Intelligent reminders
- 🏆 Achievement badges and milestone celebrations

Instead of requiring learners to manually update progress, Compass will recognize completed learning experiences and continuously adapt each learner's journey.

---

# 💼 Phase 3 — Career Services Intelligence

Compass will become an intelligent extension of Per Scholas Career Services.

### Future capabilities include:

- 📄 Resume readiness analysis
- 💻 Portfolio completion tracking
- 🎤 Interview preparation recommendations
- 🧑‍🏫 Personalized career coaching
- 🤝 Networking recommendations
- 🗓️ Employer event recommendations
- 📊 Career readiness insights

Compass will understand not only what learners know, but also how prepared they are to compete in today's technology workforce.

# 👥 Phase 3.5 — Community & Accountability

Career success is rarely achieved alone. Compass will foster a supportive community that encourages learners to stay motivated, connected, and accountable throughout their journey.

### Future capabilities include:

- 🤝 AI-matched accountability partners
- 👥 Career accountability groups
- 🎯 Shared weekly learning goals
- 📅 Progress check-ins and milestone celebrations
- 💬 Peer discussion spaces
- 🎉 Community achievement recognition
- 🌟 Alumni success stories and mentorship
- 🏆 Team learning challenges and streaks

Compass will help learners build meaningful relationships that promote consistency, encouragement, and long-term success beyond the classroom.

---

# 🌐 Phase 4 — Employer & Mentor Ecosystem

Compass will connect learners with opportunities across the Per Scholas professional network.

### Potential integrations include:

- 🏢 Employer partner opportunities
- 💼 Internship recommendations
- 🛠️ Apprenticeship pathways
- 👩‍🏫 Alumni mentor matching
- 🤝 Professional networking recommendations
- 🎟️ Industry conferences and events
- 🌍 Open-source and volunteer opportunities

Rather than functioning as a traditional job board, Compass will intelligently recommend opportunities that align with each learner's evolving skills, goals, and career readiness.

---

# 🤖 Phase 5 — Adaptive AI Career Companion

As additional learner and ecosystem data become available, Compass will transition from generating static roadmaps into continuously evolving career journeys.

### Future AI capabilities include:

- 🔄 Continuous roadmap adaptation
- 📉 Personalized skill-gap analysis
- 📈 Labor market awareness
- 🎓 Certification recommendations
- 🎯 Goal replanning
- 💬 AI coaching conversations
- 🚀 Long-term career planning
- 👑 Leadership and advancement guidance

Every learner's roadmap will evolve as their skills, experience, accomplishments, and career aspirations grow.

---

# 🏛️ Phase 6 — Per Scholas Intelligent Ecosystem

The long-term vision is for Compass to become the AI intelligence layer that connects the entire Per Scholas ecosystem.

Compass will securely integrate with systems across the learner experience to provide increasingly personalized and proactive guidance.

### Potential ecosystem integrations include:

- 🎓 Learning Management Systems
- 📚 Course progress and assessments
- 💼 Career Services
- 🤝 Mentor and Alumni Networks
- 🏢 Employer Partnerships
- 📅 Workshops and Career Events
- 📝 Resume and Portfolio Reviews
- 📊 Learner Success Analytics

Rather than asking learners to repeatedly explain where they are in their journey, Compass will understand their progress and proactively guide them toward the next meaningful opportunity.

---

# ☁️ Deployment Vision

Compass is designed to scale into a secure, cloud-based platform capable of supporting thousands of learners.

Future deployment may include:

- 🔐 Secure authentication
- 👤 Persistent learner profiles
- ☁️ Cloud-hosted AI services
- 📊 Administrative analytics dashboards
- 📱 Mobile applications
- 🔌 API integrations with Per Scholas systems
- 🏢 Employer portals
- 👩‍🏫 Mentor portals

---

# 🌍 Long-Term Vision

Compass is more than an AI application.

It is the intelligent navigation layer for the entire Per Scholas ecosystem.

By connecting learner progress, educational experiences, career services, employer partnerships, mentorship, workforce insights, and AI-powered guidance into one unified platform, Compass will deliver increasingly personalized recommendations throughout every stage of a learner's career.

Rather than asking learners to determine what to do next, Compass will continuously illuminate the path forward—empowering every Per Scholas learner with the confidence, direction, and support needed to build a successful and sustainable career in technology.

## 🧭 Our North Star

**Compass transforms data into direction, guidance into opportunity, and aspirations into successful technology careers.**
