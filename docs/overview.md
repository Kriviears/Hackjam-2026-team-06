# 🧭 Compass — Technical Overview

## Find Your Direction in Tech

---

# 📖 Project Overview

**Compass** is an AI-powered career navigation platform designed to help learners identify where they are today, define where they want to go, and receive a personalized roadmap to reach their technology career goals.

Rather than presenting learners with a generic curriculum, Compass analyzes each user's background, experience level, available study time, and career aspirations to generate a customized learning pathway. The platform guides users through clear milestones, recommended resources, practical projects, and actionable next steps.

Compass is built around the philosophy that every learner begins from a different starting point but deserves a clear direction forward.

---

# 🎯 Problem Statement

Many aspiring technology professionals struggle with questions such as:

* Where should I begin?
* Which skills should I learn first?
* What projects should I build?
* How long will it take to reach my goal?
* What should I do next?

Although countless learning resources exist, learners often lack a personalized plan that connects those resources into a meaningful progression.

Compass addresses this challenge by acting as an intelligent career navigator.

---

# 💡 Solution

Compass provides each learner with a personalized technology roadmap generated by AI.

After completing a short onboarding assessment, the platform:

* Evaluates the learner's current experience
* Identifies an appropriate technology career destination
* Determines the learner's current stage
* Creates personalized learning milestones
* Recommends the next action to take
* Presents the journey through an interactive dashboard and roadmap

The goal is to remove uncertainty and provide learners with a clear, achievable path toward a technology career.

---

# 👥 Target Users

The MVP supports three learner types:

* **Prospective Learner** — Individuals exploring technology careers or preparing to begin learning.
* **Current Learner** — Students actively developing technical skills.
* **Alumna** — Program graduates seeking continued growth, portfolio development, and career advancement.

# 🤖 AI Integration

Compass uses AI to transform learner information into a structured career roadmap.

Inputs include:

* Learner type
* Career goal
* Experience level
* Weekly time commitment

The AI analyzes these inputs and returns:

* Career destination
* Current stage
* Progress information
* Immediate next step
* Personalized learning waypoints

This allows every learner to receive recommendations tailored to their individual goals rather than following a one-size-fits-all curriculum.
